# Latent Trait Activation Report

## Activation pathways
Latent traits can activate through a deterministic weighted pathway composed of:
- environmental exposure
- trait interference threshold signal
- experience progression
- lineage drift signal
- repeated niche exposure

## Activation frequency
Activation is bounded and sparse by design (latent value gate + threshold check), so only a subset of latent channels activate per evaluation window.

## Latent → expressed transition examples
- High mobility affinity latent channel activates under repeated movement-heavy niches.
- Resonance-oriented latent channel activates when memory/mechanic interference crosses threshold.
- Survival latent channel activates when low-health exposure and drift pressure are sustained.

## Impact on novelty
By promoting previously dormant channels into expressed traits, the system increases strategy branching potential and supports higher NSER without adding broad new content systems.
