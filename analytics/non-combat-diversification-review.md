# Non-Combat Diversification Review

## Trigger/mechanic category audit
- direct combat: on_hit, on_critical, on_kill, burst damage mechanics.
- post-combat/kill-chain: streak/chain-linked triggers and finishers.
- movement/positioning: dash/reposition/velocity-coupled effects.
- memory/history: on_memory_event, lineage memory responses.
- survival/threshold: low-health, shield-threshold, comeback triggers.
- environment/season: event-conditioned seasonal mechanics.
- support/temporal/stateful: aura, buff window, delayed activations.
- non-combat progression/discovery: persistence/progression-oriented abilities.

## Findings
- Long-run winners are concentrated in direct combat and kill-chain categories.
- Memory/environment/support categories are present but underrepresented among top shares.
- Non-combat and low-combat pathways appear too low-impact or too slow to survive prolonged selection pressure.

Confidence: **moderate** (consistent with trigger/mechanic concentration across 5 runs and seasonal timeline).
