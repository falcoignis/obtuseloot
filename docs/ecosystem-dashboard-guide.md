# Ecosystem Dashboard Guide

## Panels
- **Ecosystem Health Summary Strip**: quick status for Dominance Index, Branch Entropy, Trait Variance, Lineage Concentration, and Collapse Risk.
- **Rank-Abundance Curve**: whether a few archetypes dominate or many stay active.
- **Trait Interaction Heatmap**: trait-pair co-occurrence; bright cells indicate strong pair lock-in.
- **Genome Trait Scatter Plot**: trait distribution spread and variance.
- **Lineage Survival / Concentration**: shows whether lineages remain diverse or collapse into a few dominant families.

## Healthy ecosystem signs
- Dominance Index remains moderate.
- Branch Entropy and Trait Variance stay high enough to indicate diversity.
- Heatmap shows multiple active pairings, not one single bright diagonal cluster.

## Collapse warning signs
- Dominance Index spikes while Branch Entropy drops.
- One or two lineages take most of the population.
- Heatmap shows extreme concentration (few pairings overwhelmingly bright).

## Heatmap interpretation
- Very bright `precisionAffinity × resonance` can indicate hidden precision-resonance generation bias.
- Very dim or absent `mobilityAffinity × resonance` can indicate missing interaction support.
- Heavy `chaosAffinity × volatility` concentration can indicate future collapse pressure.

## Trigger thresholds to review
- Dominance Index > 0.40
- Branch Entropy < 2.5
- Lineage Concentration > 0.20
- Collapse Risk at MEDIUM/HIGH for multiple seasonal snapshots
